Maintenance Procedure for Power Plant Generator

Generator: XYZ-123


1. Task: Preparations for Maintenance
   - Prerequisite Task: Notify the power plant supervisor for scheduled maintenance.
   - Required Tools: Safety gloves, safety goggles, flashlight, walkie-talkie.
   - Spare Parts: None.

2. Task: Power Shutdown
   - Prerequisite Task: Ensure all electrical loads are transferred to an alternative power source.
   - Required Tools: None.
   - Spare Parts: None.

3. Task: Cooling System Inspection
   - Prerequisite Task: Power Shutdown (Task 2) completed.
   - Required Tools: Pressure gauge, temperature gauge, wrench set.
   - Spare Parts: None.

4. Task: Fuel System Inspection
   - Prerequisite Task: Power Shutdown (Task 2) completed.
   - Required Tools: Fuel pressure gauge, fuel filter wrench, fuel sampling kit.
   - Spare Parts: Fuel filters (2), fuel injector nozzles (if necessary).

5. Task: Lubrication System Inspection
   - Prerequisite Task: Power Shutdown (Task 2) completed.
   - Required Tools: Oil pressure gauge, oil filter wrench, oil sampling kit.
   - Spare Parts: Oil filters (2), lubricating oil (if necessary).

6. Task: Electrical Component Inspection
   - Prerequisite Task: Power Shutdown (Task 2) completed.
   - Required Tools: Multimeter, insulation resistance tester, cable connectors.
   - Spare Parts: None.

7. Task: Generator Rotor and Stator Inspection
   - Prerequisite Task: Power Shutdown (Task 2) completed.
   - Required Tools: Megger test set, vibration analyzer, cleaning brushes.
   - Spare Parts: None.

8. Task: Belt and Pulley Inspection
   - Prerequisite Task: Power Shutdown (Task 2) completed.
   - Required Tools: Belt tension gauge, pulley alignment tool, belt dressing spray.
   - Spare Parts: Belts (if necessary).

9. Task: Exhaust System Inspection
   - Prerequisite Task: Power Shutdown (Task 2) completed.
   - Required Tools: Exhaust gas analyzer, pipe wrench, flue gas temperature probe.
   - Spare Parts: Exhaust gaskets (if necessary).

10. Task: Final Checks and Cleanup
    - Prerequisite Task: Completion of all inspections (Tasks 3-9).
    - Required Tools: Cleaning rags, lubricating oil, torque wrench.
    - Spare Parts: None.

11. Task: Power Restoration
    - Prerequisite Task: Final Checks and Cleanup (Task 10) completed.
    - Required Tools: None.
    - Spare Parts: None.
